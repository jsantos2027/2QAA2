EXTRACURRICULARS

Welcome to Averdien Montessori School, where we believe in fostering not just academic excellence
but also nurturing the boundless imagination of our students. At Averdien, we understand the
transformative power of education extends beyond the classroom, and that's why we place great
emphasis on the importance of extracurricular activities.

Our commitment to cultivating imagination is at the core of everything we do. Extracurricular
activities are not just an optional add-on; they are an integral part of the Averdien experience.
Here are some of the extrcurriculars youc an experience at Averdien

CLUBS

Averdien Montessori School boasts a vibrant extracurricular landscape designed to cater to a diverse
range of student interests. Embracing a holistic approach to education, the school offers an array of
clubs that align with its core values of fostering imagination, creativity, and physical well-being.

From the AMS Harmonics Music Club and Sonnetmaker Poetry Club to the Model United Nations of Averdien,
students can explore their passions in music, poetry, and public speaking.

The 1's and 0's Computer Science Club caters to tech enthusiasts, while the My Own Renaissance
Arts Club provides a canvas for visual arts.

For physical activity and sports enthusiasts, there are diverse options such as Goals Soccer
Club, Swish Swish Basketball Club, Blazing Trails Tennis Club, Spikes Volleyball Club,
Track & Field Titans Club, AquaeSphere Aquatics Club, Wrestle Warriors Wrestling Club, and
Grand Smash Badminton Club. Each club offers a unique space for students to engage, collaborate,
and excel, contributing to a well-rounded and enriching educational experience at Averdien Montessori School.

COMPETITIONS

Averdien Montessori School actively engages in a myriad of competitions, presenting students with
opportunities to demonstrate their talents across various domains. In the realm of arts and literature,
students participate in inter-school contests, showcasing their creative expressions in music and poetry.
The school's scientific endeavors come to life through participation in science fairs and competitions,
allowing students to present their innovative projects. The prowess of Averdien students extends to the realm
of technology, where coding competitions provide a platform for showcasing their technical skills.

Furthermore, in the realm of sports, Averdien students actively compete in inter-school leagues and tournaments,
fostering a culture of healthy competition, teamwork, and sportsmanship. These competitions not only serve as a
testament to the diverse talents within the student body but also contribute to a comprehensive and enriching
educational experience at Averdien Montessori School.
