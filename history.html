HISTORY

Averdien Montessori School established in Biringan City was founded by our previous headmistress
Taylor Alison Swift. She envisioned a school for all that prepares learners for all aspects of life;
this Averdien Montessori School was born.

In 1989, Taylor started the first school year of Averdien having a population of 50 students from Grades
7 to 12 and a faculty of only 6 teachers, she remain determined and unwavering to her commitment of
preparing students for the next generation

Tasked with giving premium high school education to its students with a carefully crafted curriculum
that aims to equip learners with not only basic education in fields of science and literacy but also
the ethics and philosophy behind being a future asset to the expanding pool of knowledge and their
future service to the country.

Up until today, Averiden has stood the test of time adjusting and constantly learning from past
experiences. We uphold the mission and vision of our school and strive to give our best as future
leaders of the nation.
