Imagination running free since 1989

On December 10, 1989 our founder, Taylor Alison Swift had a vision that all children
whether they are inclined in the sciences or pursuing the arts should have place
where they are all welcome to learn, communicate, and have fun with their peers
Thus Averdien Montessori School was born with the goal of shaping the minds of
students and creating the leaders of the next generation.

In January 3, 1999 the school established the CORE VALUES OF AN AVERY that until
today students of Averdien are actively demonstrating in all their activities.

CORE VALUES OF AN AVERY

  • A - ADAPTABILITY

    Averies can adapt to certain situations and still be able to flourish.

  • V - VERSATILE

    Excel in different fields and showcases greatness in multiple skills.

  • E - EXCELLENCE

    Uphold excellence and be the heart of exemplary virtues.

  • R - RESILIENCE

    Strongly stands and stays resilient in difficult times and challenges.

  • I - INTEGRITY

    Exercises integrity not only academically, but also in personal life.

  • E - EAGERNESS

    Always eager to learn and try new things.

  • S - SERVICE

    Service to the community and the nation.
